Study Tips

Short, practical advice you can apply today.

Pomodoro with Recall

25 min focused study + 5 min break. In the break, verbally recall what you just studied.

Active Practice

Solve at least 3 problems per concept. Check mistakes and re‑attempt.

Daily Mini‑Reviews

5–10 minutes to re‑read key formulas/diagrams before sleeping.
